During my internship, I participated in four events. Each one was a memorable experience that provided new, interesting, and useful knowledge, along with gifts and wonderful moments.

### [Event 1](4.1-Event1/)

&emsp;**Event Name:** FCAJ Community Day: Meet up

&emsp;**Date & Time:** 09:00, May 09, 2026

&emsp;**Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Tower, 02 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City

&emsp;**Role:** Attendee 

&emsp;**Brief description and main activities:** 

- Listening to experts (from FCAJ, Cloud Kinetics, VIB) share AI communication skills (standard 7-component Prompt structure, Token optimization, Chain/Tree of Thought techniques).
- Exploring real-world project architectures on AWS using the Serverless model (integrating services like CloudFront, S3, Cognito, Lambda, DynamoDB, Bedrock).
- Absorbing practical enterprise perspectives on software development mindset, professional integrity, and recruitment criteria for Freshers.

&emsp;**Results or values achieved:**

- **Technical Knowledge:** Mastering the art of Prompt Engineering to apply immediately to current projects (helping generate accurate code/documents); clearly understanding the design, pros, and cons of Serverless architecture compared to traditional infrastructure.
- **Mindset:** Absorbing the "Foundation-first" philosophy (prioritizing core foundations over chasing tools), the culture of constantly asking "Why", and the correct realization that AI is merely a productivity amplifier and cannot replace one's own critical thinking.
- **Practical Application & Soft Skills:** Enhancing proactiveness and professional integrity (independently handling edge cases); practicing a confident attitude, communication skills, teamwork, and successfully expanding the network within the IT community.
